About Me

My name is Jack Sangl. I am a Computer Engineering student at Penn State University and aspire to work in tech. I am passionate about programming and problem-solving. In 2024 I graduated from Fort LeBeouf High School with academic honors. My journey in technology began with self-driven projects inspired by Harvard’s CS50 course, where I gained foundational programming experience in C. Since then I have began my education at Penn State Behrend where I am majoring in Computer Engineering.

I have no technincal professional experience but I previously worked at Wegmans Food Markets as a Meat Customer Service Employee. This role allowed me to develop and refine my communication skills, as I consistently engaged with a diverse range of customers. I learned to adapt my approach to meet their unique needs, ensuring a positive experience and building meaningful connections.

Outside of academics, I am passionate about lifting weights, experimenting with new recipes in the kitchen, and diving into health and nutrition discussions. I believe that a healthy body fuels a productive mind, and I enjoy sharing this perspective with others.

I’m deeply excited about the ever-evolving world of technology and the opportunities it presents to make a meaningful impact. I’m eager to contribute to projects that push boundaries and improve lives through innovation. Feel free to explore my site to learn more about my work or connect with me!
